Conference Overview

The Black Engineers of Canada National Conference brings innovators, leaders, and aspiring engineers together from across the country.

Here, you will explore new opportunities, build networks, meet recruiters, and engage in insightful discussions around career growth, leadership, and representation in the engineering industry.
